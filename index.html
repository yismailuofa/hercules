Pushup Penalty

Pushups Owed:

0

Formula Explanation

P = 3*D*R - (K*1.5 + A/5)

P = Total pushups owed

D = Deaths (each death costs 3 pushups)

K = Kills (each kill reduces penalty by 1.5)

A = Assists (each assist reduces penalty by 0.2)

R = Ranged multiplier (Melee: 1.0x, Ranged: 1.25x)
